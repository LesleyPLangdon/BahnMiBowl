Bahn Mi Bowl

In your black container there is:

  • a bed of cilantro lime rice
  • strips of turkey which have been marinaded in our Bahn Mi Sauce, coated, pan fried, and drizzled with more sauce
  • vegetable potsickers

Your first step is to pop the lid to vent and microwave until hot. In my home microwave it took 2.5 minutes but yours may vary.

Remove lid and use as a plate for your potsticers.

Your Bahn Mi Bowl topping are:

  • fresh, lightly pickled cucumber
  • shredded carrot
  • sliced radish
  • chopped cilantro

This is your dipping sauce for the potstickers.

Add veggies to bowl and dipping sauce to "plate".

This is sriracha Mayo.

Drizzle sriracha Mayo over veggies. Sprinkle cilantro over bowl.

Dig in and ENJOY!!.
